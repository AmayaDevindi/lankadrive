WHAT WE OFFER

Comprehensive Automotive Services

At LankaDrive, we don't just sell vehicles — we provide a full suite of services to make ownership as smooth as the drive itself.

🏦

Vehicle Leasing Assistance

We work with BOC, HNB, Commercial Bank, and other leading institutions to secure competitive leasing packages. Our finance team guides you through eligibility, paperwork, and approval — often within 48 hours.

🛡️

Insurance Arrangements

We partner with Ceylinco, AIA, and Fairfirst Insurance to offer comprehensive motor insurance plans. Compare policies side-by-side and choose the best coverage for your vehicle category and budget.

📋

Vehicle Registration Services

Our experienced team handles all DMT documentation, revenue licensing, and number plate arrangements so you can focus on planning your first drive.

🔄

Trade-In Options

We provide fair, market-based valuations for your current vehicle. Use the trade-in value as a deposit on your next purchase, reducing the total cost and simplifying the transaction.

🚗

Test Drive Booking

Experience any vehicle in our fleet before you commit. Book a test drive at our showroom or request an at-home demo. Our sales executives will accompany you and answer every question.

🔍

Vehicle Inspection Reports

Every used and reconditioned vehicle comes with a comprehensive 150-point inspection report. We also offer independent pre-purchase inspections for vehicles you discover elsewhere.
