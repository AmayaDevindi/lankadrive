OUR STORY

Sri Lanka's Trusted Auto Partner

Founded in 2005 on Galle Road, Colombo, LankaDrive Auto Sales (Pvt) Ltd began with a simple vision: to make quality vehicles accessible to every Sri Lankan. What started as a small showroom with a handful of vehicles has grown into one of Sri Lanka's most respected auto dealerships.

We specialise in brand new imported vehicles, reconditioned vehicles sourced primarily from Japan, and certified pre-owned vehicles registered in Sri Lanka. Every vehicle in our inventory is carefully inspected, documented, and priced fairly.

Over the years, we have served more than 5,000 satisfied customers and built lasting relationships based on trust, transparency, and exceptional after-sales service.
